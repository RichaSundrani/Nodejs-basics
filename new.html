OnTheSnow

This is a business intelligence project using machine learning algorithms to assist decision making for skiers and for the ski resorts around USA.

  • Performed MLR, neural networks, regression tree, and KNN algorithms to predict snow making area to help ski resorts to prepare for the next season by predicting lift ticket price to help the skiers to plan their trip for next season.

World Development Indicators

Statistical and data analysis on The World Bank’s world development indicators dataset using R and SAS EG to find patterns and prediction in three development factors (health, education, and economy) for 171 countries.

  • Performed ANOVA and post-hoc tests to find the significance in difference in mortality rate, GDP growth, healthcare investments, literacy rate, and life expectancy.
  • Used multilinear, and logistics regression for predicting the life expectancy and GDP growth given urban population growth; adult and child mortality rate given literacy rate. The model predicts that for every 1% increase in urbanization, life expectancy for female reduces by 7 months, and for male it reduces by 9 months; for 1% increase in urbanization the GDP growth rate increases by 0.5%; for 1% increase in literacy rate the child and female mortality rate reduces by 20 to 27 deaths per thousand.
